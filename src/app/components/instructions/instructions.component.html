Quiz Instructions Read the instructions carefully
  1. Please do not click back or refresh the page when you are attempting quiz. Your responses wont be saved in that case
  2. The quizzes consists of questions carefully designed to help you self-assess your comprehension of the information presented on the topics covered in the module. No data will be collected on the website regarding your responses or how many times you take the quiz.
  3. Please go through each question in order. once you attempted an question you can navigate back to previous questions anytime
  4. Each question in the quiz is of multiple-choice format. Read each question carefully, and click on the button next to your response that is based on the information covered on the topic in the module.
  5. You have to submit response of question presented on the screen before moving to next question.Answering all the questions is mandatory to submit the quiz.
  6. After responding to a question, click on the "Next" button at the bottom to go to the next question
  7. You can navigate to any previous questions anytime to change your answer. You can make use of back button or the pagination above to do that
  8. The total score for the quiz is based on your responses to all questions. If you respond incorrectly to a question or retake a question again and get the correct response, your quiz score will reflect it appropriately. However, your quiz will not be graded, if you skip a question or exit before responding to all the questions.
  9. You can view the scores once you submit the test
